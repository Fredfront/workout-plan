🏋️‍♂️ EXERCISE TECHNIQUE GUIDE

🦵 Lower Body Exercises

Back Squat: Feet shoulder-width apart, bar on upper traps. Descend until hip crease below knee level. Drive through heels, maintain neutral spine throughout.

Romanian Deadlift (RDL): Hip-hinge movement, slight knee bend. Lower bar along legs until hamstring stretch. Drive hips forward to return.

Bulgarian Split Squat: Rear foot elevated 30cm. Lower into lunge until front thigh parallel. Drive through front heel to return.

Box Jumps: Land softly on balls of feet, absorb impact with knee flexion. Step down rather than jumping down to preserve knees.

Depth Jumps: Step off box, land and immediately jump vertically. Focus on minimal ground contact time.

💪 Upper Body Exercises

Pull-ups: Full hang to full chin over bar. Control descent, no kipping. Add weight when bodyweight exceeds 10 clean reps.

Incline DB Press: 75-80° angle, full range of motion. Lower to chest level, press to arm extension.

Bent Over Barbell Row: Hip-hinge position, bar to lower chest/upper abdomen. Squeeze shoulder blades together at top.

T-Bar Rows: Similar to barbell row but with T-bar apparatus. Focus on pulling elbows back, not just moving weight.

Arnold Press: Start with palms facing body, rotate outward while pressing overhead. Reverse the motion on descent.

🏃‍♂️ Running Technique

Proper Form: Slight forward lean from ankles, midfoot strike, cadence around 180 steps/minute.

Breathing: Rhythmic breathing - try 3:2 pattern (3 steps inhale, 2 steps exhale) for moderate efforts.

Pacing: Use a GPS watch or track. Start conservatively and build into target pace over first 400-800m.

Recovery Between Intervals: Walk or very easy jog. Heart rate should drop to ~120-130 bpm before next rep.

📋 FINAL PROGRAM NOTES & PROGRESSION TRACKING

🔄 Weekly Progression Guidelines

Strength Progression: Increase loads by 2-5% weekly during building phases. Focus on quality over quantity.

Running Progression: Paces progress systematically from aerobic base to race-specific speeds.

Pull-up Progression: Week 1-4: Build to 8+ reps, Week 5-8: Add weight or volume, Week 9-12: High volume (12+ reps)

Recovery Integration: Deload weeks (4, 8) are crucial. Use for mobility, testing, and adaptation.

📱 Recommended Apps & Tools

Running Apps: Strava, Garmin Connect, or similar GPS tracking

Interval Timer: For workout rest periods and interval training

Heart Rate Monitor: Optional but helpful for pacing guidance

Training Log: Track weights, times, and subjective feelings

Mobility Videos: All links provided lead to instructional content

⚠️ Safety & Modification Guidelines

Listen to Your Body: RPE is subjective - adjust based on daily readiness

Injury Prevention: Never skip warm-ups or cool-downs

Exercise Modifications: Can't do pull-ups? Use assisted versions or lat pulldowns

Running Surface: Vary between track, road, and treadmill for joint health

Weather Adaptations: Indoor alternatives available for all running sessions

🎓 Program Completion & Beyond

Post-Program Options:

• Repeat program with higher baseline numbers

• Focus on strength with 5/3/1 or similar program

• Specialize in running with dedicated endurance plan

• Transition to sport-specific training

Maintenance Phase: 2-3 sessions per week can maintain most gains
